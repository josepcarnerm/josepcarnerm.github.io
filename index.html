About Me

I am a graduate Computer Science student at New York University, under the supervision of Yann LeCun , currently holding a Fellowship from La Caixa. My primary research interests are Deep Learning and Computer Vision, and my current research focus is in Deep Generative Models and sequence-to-sequence models for video.

This summer I will intern at Google as a Research Software Developer, where I will be working in the Mobile Vision team.

Previously, I graduated in both Computer Science and Electrical Engineering, and spent one fantastic year working as a Machine Learning engineer in Skyscanner.

I have a strong passion for Artificial Intelligence and spend my free time building stuff and training Machine Learning models. I am also passionated about Entrepreneurship and Startups, and always keep an eye on the social impact and bussiness side of AI.
